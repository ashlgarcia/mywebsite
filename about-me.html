About Me

In the tree-ridden city of Sacramento, California, I began my journey as a tribute to the indomitable spirit instilled by a single mother's unwavering guidance. Family lies at the heart of my journey, having embraced the role of nurturing my younger brothers and stepping up when health issues rendered my mother in need. My childhood experiences, led me to embark on a shared life path with my now fiance. Our journey together has traversed the shady trees of Sacramento, vibrant lights of Las Vegas and currently settling under the warm skies of Arizona.

Now living in a sunny apartment with my fiance and two dogs and cat, I love to spend my days daydreaming of the expanses beyond our universe. With a curiousity for astrobiology and automated innovations, this newfound passion is guiding me toward the fascinating studies of planetary sciences and space exploration as a potential career and livelihood. At present, my horizons are expanding through the Correlation One Software Development program, a certification that would start my journey into data science and help prepare for the long-term goal of building a better future for generations to come.
